The Test, Learn & Grow Playbook

How to improve public services when the problem is complex, the best answer is uncertain, and you can afford to learn your way forward.

Test, Learn & Grow (TLG) is a way of working that treats delivery as a series of small, well-evaluated bets rather than one big plan committed to up front. You start by understanding the problem, try things at a manageable scale, learn honestly from what happens, and grow only what the evidence supports.

This playbook collects the principles, methods and ways of working in one place, so teams across different services can pick them up and apply them.

The three strands

Test

Try interventions at a proportionate scale, with a clear idea of what you expect to happen and how you'll know.

Learn

Evaluate openly — including when something doesn't work. Changing course on good evidence is a success, not a failure.

Grow

Feed what works back into wider practice and policy, so local learning can shape decisions at scale.

Who this is for

Anyone delivering or commissioning public-service change — service teams, analysts, policy colleagues and partners — who wants a shared language and a proportionate, evidence-led way of working.
