Case studies

Short, honest write-ups of Test, Learn & Grow in practice — what was tried, what was learned, and what happened next.

Case studies are some of the most useful content in this playbook. They turn principles into examples other teams can borrow from. A good one is candid about what didn't work, not just what did.

A simple template

Each case study answers five questions:

  1. Context. What was the problem, and for whom?
  2. What we tried. The intervention, and why we chose it.
  3. How we tested it. Scale, timescale, and how we measured.
  4. What we learned. The findings — including the surprises.
  5. What happened next. Did we grow it, adapt it, or stop?
